# Coffee-Shop-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard

1. **Project Overview**

This project analyzes coffee shop transactional sales data to uncover revenue patterns, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and peak sales periods.

An interactive data analytics dashboard was developed to transform raw sales data into actionable business insights, enabling stakeholders to optimize product strategy, staffing, and operational planning.

The dashboard highlights monthly revenue trends, peak transaction days, hourly customer traffic, product category performance, and top-selling products across multiple store locations.

2. **Business Objectives**

The main objectives of this project were to:

• Analyze **revenue growth trends across months**

• Identify **high-demand days and peak sales hours**

• Evaluate **product category contribution to revenue**

• Discover **top-performing products based on transactions and revenue**

• Compare **sales performance across store locations**

• Generate **actionable insights for operational and marketing decisions**

3. **Dashboard Highlights**

The dashboard includes the following key analytical views:

A. **Monthly Revenue Trend**

Tracks revenue performance across months to identify growth patterns and seasonal demand.

**Key Insights**

• Revenue increased from **$81,678 in January to $166,486 in June**

• **~104% revenue growth** observed within six months

• **May and June recorded the highest sales**, indicating strong mid-year demand

**Business Insight**

Coffee demand increases significantly during late spring and early summer, which may require **inventory planning and marketing campaigns during these months**.

B. **Transactions by Day of Week**

Analyzes transaction volume across different days to identify peak demand days.

**Key Insights**

| Day       | Transactions |
| --------- | ------------ |
| Friday    | 21,701       |
| Thursday  | 21,654       |
| Monday    | 21,643       |
| Wednesday | 21,310       |
| Tuesday   | 21,202       |
| Sunday    | 21,096       |
| Saturday  | 20,510       |

**Business Insight**

• **Thursday and Friday are the busiest days**, suggesting strong weekday demand.

• **Saturday shows the lowest transactions**, indicating potential opportunity for weekend promotions.

C. **Customer Traffic by Hour**

Identifies peak store activity hours.

**Key Insights**

• Peak traffic occurs between **8 AM – 10 AM**

• Highest activity recorded at **10 AM (~18,545 transactions)**

• Traffic significantly drops after **11 AM**

**Business Insight**

Morning hours are the primary revenue window, suggesting:

• Increased staffing between **8 AM – 10 AM**

• Promotional breakfast combos during this time

D. **Product Category Contribution**

Evaluates which product categories generate the most sales.

| Category           | Transactions |
| ------------------ | ------------ |
| Coffee             | 58,416       |
| Tea                | 45,449       |
| Bakery             | 22,796       |
| Drinking Chocolate | 11,468       |
| Flavours           | 6,790        |

**Key Insight**

Coffee and Tea together contribute **~70% of total sales**, making them the core revenue drivers.

**Business Insight**

• Coffee and tea inventory management is critical.

• Upselling strategies could focus on **pairing bakery items with beverages**.

E. **Top Selling Products**

| Product               | Transactions | Revenue |
| --------------------- | ------------ | ------- |
| Brewed Chai Tea       | 17,183       | $77,082 |
| Gourmet Brewed Coffee | 16,912       | $70,035 |
| Barista Espresso      | 16,403       | $91,406 |
| Hot Chocolate         | 11,468       | $72,416 |
| Brewed Black Tea      | 11,350       | $47,932 |

**Key Insights**

• **Barista Espresso generates the highest revenue ($91K+)**

• **Brewed Chai Tea is the most purchased product**

• Beverage products dominate top sales positions

**Business Insight**

• Premium coffee drinks generate higher revenue per transaction

• Marketing campaigns could focus on **signature beverages**

6. **Key Business Insights**

• Revenue grew **104% from January to June**

• **Morning hours (8–10 AM)** are peak sales windows

• **Thursday and Friday** generate the highest transactions

• **Coffee & Tea contribute ~70% of total sales**

7. **Business Recommendations**

A. **Optimize Morning Operations**

Increase staffing and inventory during **8–10 AM** peak hours to reduce service time and increase throughput.

B. **Launch Weekend Promotions**

Introduce discounts or bundles to improve **Saturday sales performance**.

C. **Beverage Upselling Strategy**

Bundle **coffee with bakery products** to increase average order value.

D. **Promote High-Margin Products**

Highlight premium drinks like **Barista Espresso** through targeted promotions.

E. **Inventory Optimization**

Focus supply planning on **coffee and tea products**, which drive the majority of revenue.
